김치찌개 (kimchi-jjigae) Recipe

Description

김치찌개 (kimchi-jjigae) or kimchi stew is a popular Korean dish made with kimchi, tofu, and other ingredients, such as pork, scallions, onions. It's believed to have originated in the mid-Joseon era (~1500s).

Ingredients

Servings: 2

    Meat

  • 0.5lb pork belly, bite size pieces
  • 1tbsp mirin
  • Black pepper

    • Kimchi & Other Ingredients

  • 3/4c aged kimchi, bite size pieces
  • 1/4 small yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 stalk green onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 shiitake mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 150g firm tofu, sliced
  • 1c water

    • Broth

  • 1tbsp 고춧가루 (gochugaru)
  • 1tbsp soy sauce
  • 1tsp 고추장 (gochujang)
  • Minced garlic
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Marinate pork belly in mirin & black pepper for 15m
  2. Cook kimchi until soft
  3. Add marinated pork belly, kimchi, onion, mushrooms, tofu, water, and broth sauce
  4. Bring to a boil then lower to medium heat for another 10-15m
  5. Top with sliced green onions
